Identifying Malignancy of Lung Cancer using Deep Learning


To download the paper Click here (PDF).

Most literature related to identifying tumors focus either on classifying based on types such as adenoma, carcinoma, teratoma etc or classify the malignancy as benign or malignant. But there are some tumors that are not cancerous yet, but have the potential to become cancerous. Such tumors are pre-malignant. Hence, this project focuses on improving the efficiency in identifying premalignant tumors.

The research paper associated to this was published in the Springer Artificial Intelligence for Healthcare book on Oct 30, 2021.
